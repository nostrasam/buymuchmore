Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: 17 September, 2024

At ShopMuchMore, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This document outlines how you can request the deletion of your personal information from our platform.

What Does Data Deletion Mean?

Data deletion refers to the process of permanently removing your personal data from our systems. Once your data has been deleted, it cannot be retrieved or restored.

How to Request Data Deletion:

You can request the deletion of your personal data by following these steps:

1. Login to Your Account:

• Go to the BuyMuchMore website and log in to your account.

2. Submit a Data Deletion Request:

• Once logged in, navigate to the contact us page at the footer of the website.

• Click on the contact us link and fill in the necessary fields and write a clear statement requesting to delete your data on the message box.

• - Click on this option and follow the on-screen instructions to confirm your request.

3. Email Request:

• If you are unable to access the contact us page, you may also request data deletion by sending an email to our Data Protection Officer at dpo@buymuchmore.co.uk.

• In your email, include the following information:

• Your full name.

• The email address associated with your ShopMuchMore account.

• Your telephone.

• A clear statement requesting the deletion of your data.

4. Verification:

• To ensure the security of your data, we may ask you to verify your identity by providing some additional information.

5. Processing Time:

• Once we receive your request, we will process it within **30 days** as required by applicable data protection regulations.

• You will receive an email confirmation once your data has been permanently deleted from our systems.

Data That May Not Be Deleted Immediately:

Please note that certain data may not be deleted immediately due to legal or regulatory requirements, such as:

• Transaction Records: For example, if you've made purchases on the platform, we may need to retain some information for accounting, tax, or legal compliance purposes.

• Fraud Prevention: Some data may also be retained for fraud prevention or security monitoring purposes for a limited period.

Consequences of Data Deletion:

Once your data has been deleted:

• You will no longer have access to your BuyMuchMore account.

• All personal information associated with your account, including purchase history, saved items, and preferences, will be permanently removed.

• Any active subscriptions, memberships, or services linked to your account will be terminated.

If you have any concerns or require further assistance with the data deletion process, please contact our customer support team at support@buymuchmore.co.uk

FAQs on Data Deletion

Q: Can I recover my account after data deletion?

A: No, once your data is deleted, it cannot be recovered. You will need to create a new account if you wish to use our services again.

Q: What happens to my order history?

A: All order history and personal details will be permanently deleted, but we may retain anonymized transaction records for legal purposes.

Q: How long does the data deletion process take?

A: It typically takes up to 30 days to fully process a data deletion request.
